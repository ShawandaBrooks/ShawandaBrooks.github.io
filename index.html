About me

I Am Shawanda, a

My name is Shawanda, but you can call me Sha! I was born and raised in Providence, RI. I am a creative and love all things design. I am lover of learning and someone who does a lot of self-studying.

I spend my free-time researching, reading, and expanding my knowledge; trying to better understand the unknown. I am passionate about writing and the flexbility with having creative freedom. I enjoy spontaneous adventures and love all things thriller!

With the impact of COVID-19, the pandemic left me with plenty of spare time. I became overly driven to develop a new skill so I self-explored web development. I became fascinated with the idea of learning this new fond language- I had to expand my knowledge. I attended an accelerated program with an emphasis on HTML, CSS, and Javascript, also touching on Bootstrap. My main interest is in front-end development and with more experience and my learning in the back-end, that is becoming of interest as well.

My goal is to be upon the group of females that bridge the gender gap in tech! Girls Code!
